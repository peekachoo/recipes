Edible Cookie Dough

Description

I've made this 3 times. The first and second I didn't really like it. But the third time it turned out super chewy and delicious and I liked it very much. But the reason why I don't make it much is because it's more labor-demanding than making cookies (yes it is!).

Ingredients

  • AP flour
  • Milk
  • Granulated sugar (seriously don't use big grained sugar the texture would suck!)
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 150*C. Bake the flour for 5 minutes. Stir up mid-way.
  2. In the meantime, prepare the wet ingredients.
  3. Mix up melted butter, sugar and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine the wet ingredients with flour and salt.
  5. Add milk to adjust the texture until it reaches the desired consistency.
  6. Add in chocolate chips and continue to mix.
  7. (Mandatory!) (Preshape if wanted) Cover carefully and let chill in the fridge for at least 1 day.
  8. Enjoy with ice scream or a cup of milk :).
