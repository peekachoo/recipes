Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

I am obsessed with this recipe. I've been making cookies almost every week ever since I've done it the first time. And sometimes I can't sleep at night because I have a cookie craving...

Ingredients

  • 100gr melted butter.
  • 60gr brown sugar.
  • 40gr granulated sugar.
  • 1tsp of vanilla extract.
  • 1 whole egg.
  • 1tsp of baking soda.
  • 1tsp of salt.
  • 180gr of AP flour.
  • Chocolate chips.

Steps

  1. Mix butter, sugar just until well-combined.
  2. Add 1 egg and vanilla extract and continue to mix.
  3. Mix AP flour, baking soda and salt in another bowl.
  4. Sift the mixed flour into the wet ingredients.
  5. Mix until well-combined. Be careful not to overmix it.
  6. Add in chocolate chips and continue to mix.
  7. (Optional but highly recommended) Cover carefully and chill the dough in the fridge for at least 1 hour (preferably a day).
  8. Preheat the oven to 180*C. Bake in 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Enjoy with a cup of milk :).
