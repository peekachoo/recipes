Dorayaki

Description

Made once. Turned out super beautiful even when I poured inconsistent amount of batter. The taste is good but it's not as mesmerizing as cookies and cookie dough. I'm pretty sure half of it is because I was busy playing games while eating and the other half is chocolate is better than anything. Maybe I should try chocolate filling next time!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • AP flour
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Powder
  • Red Beans
  • Sugar
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

Fillings:

  1. Cook the red beans until soft.
  2. Drain the water.
  3. Smash the red beans.
  4. Add sugar and mix until well-combined.

Pancakes:

  1. Mix 2 eggs with sugar, honey and vanilla extract.
  2. Sift in the batter mixed flour with salt and baking powder.
  3. Mix until well-combined.
  4. Add 10ml of water and continue to mix.
  5. Cover and let rest for about 10-15 minutes.
  6. Pour 1 spoonful of batter to a preheat non-stick pan in low-med temperature (to create that beautiful color!).
  7. Cook for about 2-3 minutes till brown.
  8. Flip and cook the other side for 1 minute.
